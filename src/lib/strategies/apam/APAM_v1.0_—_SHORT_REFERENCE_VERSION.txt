APAM v1.0 — SHORT REFERENCE VERSION
(For quick human review or as a “cheat sheet”)

Core Concepts
Zones: DBR/RBR = Demand, RBD/DBD = Supply

Proximal = median body of base; Distal = median wick of structure

Base = 1–6 candles, wick > 2× body, rejection > 1 ATR

Trend = EMA(20) slope + HH/HL structure

Momentum = % of HTF swing range + RSI divergence

Scoring (10 Points)
Strength (2): breakout > 2× ATR

Time (1): 1–3 candles

Freshness (1.5): untested in 20 bars

Trend (2): with trend

Momentum (1.5): extreme + divergence

Reward (2): >4:1 to next zone
Minimum score to trade: 7

Execution
≥8.5 score → Limit‑Edge Entry

7–8 score → Confirmation Entry

<7 → No trade

Risk
1% per trade

Stop = distal ± 1–2× ATR

Partial exits: 50% at 1:1, 25% at 2:1, trail rest

Time Frames
HTF = trend/momentum

MTF = zones

LTF = entries
